What it is

A measurement tool for institutional health.

Museum boards and executive directors make capital decisions — programming investments, capital campaigns, staffing changes — without normalized data to guide them. The MVI changes that.

Using public data, Form 990 filings, social media metrics, and client-supplied figures, Museum Planning LLC scores your institution against a benchmark database of comparable museums. The result is a clear, actionable picture of where you are strong, where you are vulnerable, and what to do next.

No competing product offers a normalized, ratio-based diagnostic that scales from a $300K community museum to a $25M national institution.

Five dimensions of institutional health

01

Visitor Experience

Attendance trends, repeat visitation, TripAdvisor signals, program diversity, and accessibility indicators.

02

Digital Presence

Website quality, search visibility, social media engagement, and digital content strategy.

03

Financial Health

Revenue diversification, earned income ratios, endowment strength, and operating reserve sustainability.

04

Community Impact

School and community partnerships, earned media, civic relationships, and educational program reach.

05

Institutional Strength

Governance structure, staff depth, collections stewardship, and accreditation standing.
